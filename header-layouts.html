3. Two-Row

Layout Analysis & Recommendations

1. Minimal

Pros

  • Maximum content space
  • Clean, focused aesthetic
  • Fast visual parsing
  • Logo remains recognizable

Cons

  • Loses app name visibility
  • Still 4 elements in one row
  • Edit button competes with icons

Best for

Users who open the app frequently and don't need branding reinforcement. Great for power users.

2. FAB Pattern

Pros

  • Camera always accessible
  • Cleaner header (3 elements)
  • Maintains brand presence
  • Modern, familiar pattern
  • Camera action is prominent

Cons

  • FAB occludes content
  • Less iOS-native (more Material)

Best for

Emphasizing camera capture as the primary action. Best balance of clarity and functionality.

3. Two-Row

Pros

  • Clear hierarchy
  • Most breathing room
  • Space for filters/context
  • Actions clearly separated

Cons

  • Takes most vertical space
  • May feel heavy on mobile

Best for

Apps with filtering/sorting needs or when you want maximum clarity and hierarchy.
