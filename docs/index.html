#contact

A business card or a phone number on a napkin. Photograph it. QuillStack fills in the name, number, email, and opens your Contacts app with fields pre-filled.

opens iOS Contacts
#event

A flyer on a telephone pole. A date, a time, a place. Photograph it. QuillStack finds what matters and opens your calendar. The event is already there.

opens Calendar
#todo

A list you wrote this morning. Photograph it. QuillStack pulls each task and sends them to Apple Reminders.

opens Reminders
#receipt

A paper receipt. Photograph it. QuillStack reads the vendor, the total, the line items and exports a clean summary to your notes.

exports to Obsidian
#meeting

Your meeting notes. Photograph them. QuillStack organizes what you wrote — attendees, agenda, action items — into something you can use.

structured note

A thought you caught on paper before it disappeared. Photograph it. QuillStack stores it, searchable, dated, waiting for when you need it again.
