What it does

One-tap capture

The document scanner auto-crops and corrects perspective; multi-page captures stack into one item.

Search by content

On-device text recognition reads every capture, so you can find “that Delta ticket from March” by what it says — not just by date. Images never leave your phone.

On-device enrichment

A model on your device reads each capture and pulls out a title, suggested tags, and quick actions. None of it leaves your phone.

Tags, not folders

Ten curated tags cover the shape of everyday paper. No nested folders to reorganize every six months.

Quick actions

Turn a business card into a contact, an event flyer into a calendar entry, a to-do list into Reminders.

Obsidian export

Point QuillStack at your vault. Captures export as markdown with images attached.

On-device by default

Everything you capture is processed and stored on your device. Text recognition and enrichment run entirely on your device. Your content never leaves unless you choose iCloud sync or Obsidian export. The only thing sent off-device is pseudonymous crash diagnostics, never your images or notes. Full detail in the privacy policy.
